Managed/Unmanaged Virtual Servers in the Cloud


In today's computing market, many companies are turning to Cloud based solution for the infrastructure for many different reasons.
Whether you're looking to transfer your entire infrastructure or briefly setup a virtual testing lab, our servers are the perfect solution for any problem or project.

We are set apart from other providers because we understand that while having managed service is nice, many users looking into cloud computing are knowledgable administrators that are more than capable of setting up and maintaining servers on their one.

Because of this:
  • We allow you to purchase unmanaged services at a cheaper price, while still providing any support needed to setup and maintain the systems for free
  • We offer managed support, allowing you to leave both setup and maintanence completely in our hands, alleviating any stress from yours
  • We provide free monitoring support and alerts, and respond to them 24/7 should you wish for us to do so
  • You can get ahold of support 24/7 for any assistance

Our Servers Come With Top of the Line Feature, Including:

Instant provisioning via our use of the Openstack API and custom built backends and control panels depending on the need you, the customer.
Redundancy built into every system via our RAID 10 storage systems on SSD Drives, multiple LAN ports, ECC RAM, and matching Power Supplies on every server.
Secure environments that are constantly tested for security holes and free DDOS protection.
1GBPS Networking bonded to each VM / Dedicated machine, allowing for blazing fast connections to your Block Storage or Databases.
